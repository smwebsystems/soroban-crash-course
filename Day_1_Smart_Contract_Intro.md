## Day 1 - A Guide to Smart Contract Development on Stellar
---

### Objective
To equip participants with a strong conceptual and technical understanding of how smart contracts work on the Stellar network using Soroban, and to enable them to confidently design, deploy, and interact with secure, efficient, and scalable smart contracts.
